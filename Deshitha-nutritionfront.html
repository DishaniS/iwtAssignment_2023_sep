  • 14 Day Heart and Healthy Plans


    Day 1-3:
    Start your day with oatmeal topped with berries for breakfast. For lunch, enjoy a salad with grilled chicken or fish. Dinner can include grilled salmon, vegetables, and brown rice.
    Day 4-7:
    Swap coffee for green tea and snack on nuts. Include colorful salads, lean proteins, and whole grains in your meals.
    Day 8-11:
    Kickstart mornings with a brisk walk. Include a mix of fruits, veggies, and lean proteins in your meals. Stay hydrated and cut back on added sugars.
    Day 12-14:
    Incorporate strength training exercises and relaxation techniques. Opt for lean proteins like chicken or beans and limit salt intake.
